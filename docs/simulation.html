What to Watch For

Formation Convergence

Seven drones start in a planar wedge. The formation error decays as the LQR controller converges. At t=8s, the offsets smoothly morph to a 3D diamond -- watch the error spike and recover.

Obstacle Negotiation

The canyon corridor is defined by two building blocks (gray wireframes) with three pillar obstacles (purple wireframes) inside. APF repulsion forces push drones away from walls and pillars.

Wind Gust Rejection

Dryden-model turbulence (sigma=2 m/s, tau=5s) perturbs each drone independently. The consensus force from neighboring agents pulls displaced drones back into formation.

Ghost Targets

Small wireframe spheres show the desired formation offset positions relative to the leader. The gap between a drone and its ghost visualizes the instantaneous formation error.

Metric Plots

The four charts below show the full simulation history. The formation morph at t=8.0s is visible as a spike in the formation error and control effort plots.
