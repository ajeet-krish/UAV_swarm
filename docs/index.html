2D APF Slalom Path Planning

APF-based navigation through a dense 15-obstacle slalom corridor. Ten gain values from 0.05 to 10 reveal four distinct behavioral regimes: corner-cutting at low gain, local equilibrium stall at intermediate gain, optimal slalom at k_a = 4, and saturating repulsion at high gain. The full analysis includes gain sweep overlay, faceted comparison, clearance trade-study heatmap, gate performance dashboard, speed-space phase portrait, and Monte Carlo safety envelope.

3D Formation Flight & Obstacle Negotiation

Decentralized formation control of seven quadcopters flying a 3D DCM heading-aligned wedge through 15 spherical debris obstacles. LQR optimal control drives state feedback, graph Laplacian consensus maintains formation cohesion, and the Dryden turbulence model injects realistic wind gusts. The interactive Three.js viewer provides orbit controls, playback timeline, and per-drone neon trail visualization.
